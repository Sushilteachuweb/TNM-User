TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Build Fails - "Keystore not found":
   Solution: Verify path in android/key.properties

Build Fails - "Wrong password":
   Solution: Check passwords in android/key.properties

Build Fails - "Duplicate class":
   Solution: Run "flutter clean" and rebuild

App Crashes on Launch:
   Solution: Check logs with "adb logcat | grep Flutter"
   Solution: Test on physical device, not emulator
   Solution: Rebuild with "flutter clean"

Upload Rejected - "Duplicate package":
   Solution: Ensure creating NEW app in Play Console

Upload Rejected - "Missing privacy policy":
   Solution: Add privacy policy URL in Play Console
